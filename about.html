About Me

Welcome to the About page, where I will share a bit about me!

Who Am I?

My full name is Ava Joan Ligorio and I am from the "Sun City" -- El Paso, Texas. I do enjoy the Southwest -- despite its dryness and heat! I am currently twenty years old. I love movies and music. I listen to music most hours of the day -- I believe it is what keeps the world revolving! I have one sister named Francesca and four cat siblings: Sloane, Thomas, Felix, and Earl.

In terms of professional goals, I am unsure of the future at the moment. I do hope to be successful and comfortable in the future -- and, most importantly -- doing something that I enjoy. I do know what is important to me now: my loved ones and spending as much time as I can living in each present moment. That is something I struggle with -- being present. I am oftentimes preoccupied by the past or the future -- I think we all should focus on the very important now.

My Life's Love

In the absence of a love of love today, my greatest dream is to make life a little bit easier for all of us. I think we are all wondrous creatures with a lot to offer each other. Life is a profound experience. It is difficult! To quote the great Stevie Wonder: "Love's in need of love today." Then, to quote the great Joni Mitchell: "We are stardust / we are golden!" We are the smallest of particles, and we are also sparkly because of that!
